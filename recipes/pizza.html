Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarrella Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Pre-Made Pizza Dough

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Roll out dough into a circular pan.
  3. Spread even layer of sauce onto the dough.
  4. Sprinkle even layer of cheese atop sauce.
  5. Add pepperonis to taste.
